FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our services, process, and rental policies. Let us clarify your doubts!

What types of furniture can I rent from Rinterio?

We offer a wide range of furniture, including living room sets, bedroom furniture, office pieces, and outdoor furnishings.

How long is the rental period?

Our rental periods are flexible, ranging from short-term (1 month) to long-term (12 months or more), depending on your needs.

What happens if an item gets damaged during the rental period?

Minor wear and tear is expected, but significant damage may incur a repair fee. Contact us immediately to discuss any issues.

Can I extend my rental period?

Yes, you can easily extend your rental period by contacting our customer support team before your current contract ends.

Do you offer delivery and setup services?

Yes, we provide free delivery and setup for all rentals within our service area. Our team ensures a hassle-free experience.
